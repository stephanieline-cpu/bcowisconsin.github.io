Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 12, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

Benefits Counseling of Wisconsin respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We only collect information that you choose to share with us, such as:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number

You may share this information when you contact us by email or phone.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Answer your questions
  • Provide benefits counseling services
  • Communicate with you

We do not sell or share your personal information.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to work properly. Cookies do not collect personal information.

You can turn off cookies in your browser settings.

Keeping Your Information Safe

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. No website can guarantee complete security, but we work to keep your information safe.

Accessibility

Benefits Counseling of Wisconsin is committed to accessibility. If you have trouble accessing our website or this policy, please contact us and we will help.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if needed. Any changes will be posted on this page.
